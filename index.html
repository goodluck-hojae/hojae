Hojae Son

Amherst, Massachusetts

Hi, I’m Hojae Son, a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at UMass Amherst, advised by Professor Marco Serafini. My research interests are in systems for machine learning, especially LLM inference, KV cache optimization, vector databases, and graph neural network systems. I am also working as a Research Computing Assistant for Unity Cluster.
